Reborn version of lucifekit's sword's story war 3 maps.

HOW TO PLAY : 
- Move with left click. ( RPG style )
- Attack with left or right click. ( RPG style )
- Hold ctrl and left click to jump.

OBJECTIVE : 
- Choose a hero and kill creep to level up.
- Destroy and secure tower on the map for exp.
- Kill other team's heroes and bosses.
- First team reach 50 kills will win.

Tips : Buy mango for easy start.
